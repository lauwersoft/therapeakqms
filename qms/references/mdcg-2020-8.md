# MDCG 2020-8 - Post-Market Clinical Follow-up (PMCF) Evaluation Report Template

April 2020

This document has been endorsed by the Medical Device Coordination Group (MDCG) established by Article 103 of Regulation (EU) 2017/745. The MDCG is composed of representatives of all Member States and it is chaired by a representative of the European Commission. The document is not a European Commission document and it cannot be regarded as reflecting the official position of the European Commission. Any views expressed in this document are not legally binding and only the Court of Justice of the European Union can give binding interpretations of Union law.

## 1. Purpose

This document provides a template for the PMCF Evaluation Report as required by EU MDR 2017/745 Annex XIV Part B. The template aims to harmonise the format and content of PMCF evaluation reports across manufacturers and facilitate assessment by notified bodies.

## 2. Scope

This template applies to all medical devices subject to the MDR that require post-market clinical follow-up activities as part of their post-market surveillance obligations.

## 3. Template Structure

The PMCF Evaluation Report should include the following sections:

### 3.1 Device Description and Specification

- Device identification (name, model, version, UDI)
- Description of intended purpose
- Target population and medical conditions
- Description of the technology

### 3.2 Reference to Applicable Documents

- PMCF Plan
- Clinical Evaluation Report
- Post-Market Surveillance Plan and Report
- Risk Management File

### 3.3 Summary and Appraisal of Clinical Data

- Summary of PMCF activities performed during the reporting period
- Summary and appraisal of clinical data collected
- Analysis of data against the objectives defined in the PMCF plan

### 3.4 Impact on the Clinical Evaluation

- Assessment of whether the collected data confirms or changes the conclusions of the clinical evaluation
- Assessment of the benefit-risk profile in light of new data
- Identification of any new risks or changes to known risks

### 3.5 Conclusions and Actions

- Overall conclusions on the clinical performance and safety of the device
- Description of any corrective or preventive actions taken or planned
- Assessment of the need to update the clinical evaluation, risk management, or post-market surveillance plan
- Recommendation on the continuation, modification, or cessation of PMCF activities

## 4. Reporting Frequency

PMCF evaluation reports should be updated:

- At least annually for Class III and implantable devices
- At intervals appropriate to the device risk class for other devices (typically annually or as defined in the PMCF plan)
