# Usability Engineering

Documentation that provides evidence of the usability engineering activities and processes conducted in accordance with state-of-the-art usability engineering standards and regulations for medical devices.

## Applicable Regulatory Standards

The following are the key applicable regulatory standards and technical reports for usability engineering under EU MDR:

- IEC 62366-1:2015
- IEC/TR 62366-2:2016

## Expected Usability Engineering Documents

The following are the expected documentation for usability engineering under EU MDR:

- Use scenarios and use errors
- Usability engineering plans
- Usability engineering reports

## Expected Traceability Evidence

It is essential that the usability engineering documentation above is supported by traceability evidence to demonstrate the completeness of usability engineering activities and documentation from use scenarios and use errors, through to usability engineering plans, summative and formative evaluation activities and reports. Usability traceability must also demonstrate the interplay between usability engineering and risk management documentation.

## Desired Submission Format

Scarlet specifies desired submission formats for certain technical documentation to facilitate easier assessment. It is recommended that usability traceability is documented as multiple traceability matrices in tabular format, such as .XLSX.

## Expected Traceability

The following are the expected traceability for usability engineering under EU MDR:

- **Evaluation of use scenarios and use errors:**
  - Use scenarios and use errors -> Usability engineering summative evaluation activities and reports
- **Contribution of use errors to hazardous situations:**
  - Use errors -> Hazardous situations
- **Risk control effectiveness verification, where applicable:**
  - Risk controls -> Usability engineering summative evaluation reports
