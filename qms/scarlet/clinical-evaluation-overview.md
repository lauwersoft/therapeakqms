# Clinical Evaluation

Documentation that details evidence generation activities to substantiate clinical benefit and performance claims.

## Applicable Regulatory Standards

The following are the key applicable regulatory standards for clinical evaluation under EU MDR:

- EU Medical Device Regulation (MDR) 2017/745
- MEDDEV 2.7/1 Rev. 4
- MDCG 2020-5

## Expected Clinical Documentation

The following are the expected documentation for clinical evaluation under EU MDR:

- Clinical evaluation plan
- Literature searches
- Equivalence claims
- Clinical investigation plan
- Clinical investigation report
- Clinical evaluation report
- Summary of safety and clinical performance

## Clinical Foundations: Clinical Benefits and State of the Art (SOTA)

This first milestone focuses on defining how your device benefits patients and how it aligns with state-of-the-art (SOTA) medical practice. It includes:

- Clinical evaluation planning.
- Equivalence.
- Data identification.

## Clinical Outputs: Clinical Data and Post-market Surveillance (PMS)

This second milestone focuses on generating and analysing clinical data to support your device's safety and performance.

- Data generation.
- Data appraisal & analysis.
- Clinical evaluation report.
