# Risk Management

Documentation that details risk management activities to establish the acceptability of benefit-risk ratio.

## Applicable Regulatory Standards

- ISO 14971:2019
- IEC 62366-1
- MDCG 2019-16
- IEC 81001-5-1

## Expected Risk Documentation

- Risk management plan
- Device characteristics
- Risk management file
- Risk management report

## Deconstructing the Risk Management Process

Scarlet is uniquely designed to guide you through every step of the process, ensuring you:

- Work through the two key milestones of clinical evaluation.
  - Risk analysis
  - Risk control and evaluation
  - Overall residual risk acceptable
- Receive step-by-step guidance on methodologies to identify risks and strategies to control them.
- Ensure regulatory alignment by structuring your evaluation in compliance with ISO 14971.

By following Scarlet's structured approach, you can confidently prepare a high-quality risk management submission that meets all regulatory and safety expectations.

## Overview of the Risk Management Process

### Milestone 1: Risk Analysis

- Risk management plan established.
- Hazards, hazardous situations and subsequent harms identified.
- Risks estimated (severity and probability).

### Milestone 2: Risk Control and Evaluation

- Risk acceptability criteria applied.
- Risk control measures implemented and verified.
- Residual risks evaluated and documented.

### Milestone 3: Overall Residual Risk Acceptable

- Overall residual risk judged acceptable.
- Risk-benefit analysis confirms benefits outweigh risks.
- Risk management report approved.
- Post-production surveillance established.
