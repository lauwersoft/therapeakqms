# Technical Documentation: Applicable Regulatory Standards

The table below provides an overview of Scarlet's technical documentation workstreams, relevant regulatory standards, and the topics covered in each workstream.

## Workstreams Overview

### Device Information

**Relevant regulatory standards:**
- EU MDR
- ISO 15233-1:2021
- ISO 20417:2021
- Regulation (EU) 2021/2226

**Regulatory topics:**
- Device & risk classification
- Device description
- Intended purpose
- Labelling
- Instructions for Use
- CE marking
- Declaration of conformity

### Risk / Usability

**Relevant regulatory standards:**
- EU MDR
- ISO 14971:2019
- ISO TR 24971:2020
- IEC 62366-1:2015+A1:2020
- IEC TR 62366-2:2016

**Regulatory topics:**
- Risk management planning
- Device characteristics
- Risk analysis
- Risk evaluation
- Risk controls
- Residual risk evaluation
- Benefit-risk analysis
- Risk management reporting
- Risk management process review
- User training
- Usability engineering formative evaluation planning
- Usability engineering formative evaluation reporting
- Usability engineering summative evaluation planning
- Usability engineering summative evaluation reporting

### Software

**Relevant regulatory standards:**
- EU MDR
- IEC 82304-1:2016
- IEC 62304:2006+A1:2015
- IEC 81001-5-1:2021

**Regulatory topics:**
- Use requirements
- Software development planning
- Software architecture & detailed design
- AI model design
- Software verification activities
- Software test specifications & execution reports
- Software verification reporting
- Software release
- Software risk management
- Software problem resolution
- Software change management
- Validation planning
- Validation reporting

### Clinical

**Relevant regulatory standards:**
- EU MDR
- MEDDEV 2.7/1
- ISO 14155
- MDCG guidance**

**Regulatory topics:**
- Clinical benefits
- Equivalence claim
- Clinical evaluation planning
- Literature search & SOTA
- Clinical investigation planning
- Clinical investigation reporting
- Clinical evaluation reporting
- Summary of safety and clinical performance
- Post-market clinical follow-up
- Post-market surveillance

### Technical Documentation Content

**Relevant regulatory standards:**
- EU MDR

**Regulatory topics:**
- GSPR checklist
- Traceability documentation*

---

\* Encompassing end-to-end traceability across technical documentation artefacts in the Device Information, Risk, Software and Clinical workstreams.

\*\* Manufacturers should be aware of the MDCG guidance documents. While not all documents apply to every device or clinical evaluation process, subsequent knowledge base articles on clinical topics will reference relevant guidance where appropriate.

The chosen international standards provided in this table are a snapshot of the current state of the art in medical device risk management, usability engineering, and software development. This state-of-the-art is subject to change and is focused only on providing general, foundational guidance on software as a medical device development. The manufacturer is still responsible for ensuring they stay abreast of the changing regulatory landscape and incorporate additional standards relevant to their medical device.
