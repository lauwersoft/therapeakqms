# The Scarlet Way

Explains how you interact with Scarlet: the key milestones and tools available to you.

## How We Work Together

We keep things clear and move fast:

- Your dedicated Account Manager (AM) coordinates all Scarlet engagement end-to-end. One point of contact, zero handoffs.
- We follow a steady cadence (weekly/bi-weekly) to keep momentum and make progress visible.
- Every touchpoint ends with clear owners, actions, and timelines, so nothing stalls between stages.

## The Key Milestones

The exact sequence depends on your scope and device class, but most engagements include these stages:

1. **Prepare:** We align on scope and regulatory expectations so you know what "good" looks like before you submit.
2. **Submit:** You complete the application form and upload the expected documentation in the Scarlet web app.
3. **Assess:** We audit your Quality Management System and/or assess your medical device technical documentation.
4. **Final review and decision making:** If approved, we issue the certificate.
5. **Comply:** We support ongoing requirements, including surveillance, change requests, and post-market obligations.

## The Scarlet Toolkit

Here are the resources available to you as a Scarlet customer, organised by when they are most useful:

### Always Available

- **Check-in sessions:** A weekly/biweekly 15-20 min session with your AM to share progress and answer questions.
- **Knowledge base:** Articles and videos that explain various regulatory concepts and expectations.
- **Chat/email:** Use this to unblock small issues or answer quick questions without waiting for scheduled calls.
- **Structured dialogues:** Focused conversations with assessors to clarify regulatory expectations and reduce uncertainty. Conversations will be focused on "what needs to be fulfilled" rather than "how to fulfil". Learn more in the Structured Dialogues article.

### Prepare

- **Coverage checker:** In-app tooling that provides directional feedback by highlighting where expected concepts appear in your documentation. This helps apply the best practices and guidance from the knowledge base more effectively. Learn more in the Coverage Check Analysis article.
- **Copy prompt:** In-app tooling that generates a ready-to-use prompt you can paste into your preferred LLM tool (along with the relevant documents from your technical file) to quickly check how your documentation aligns with specific articles in Scarlet's Knowledge Base.

### Assess

- **Clarification calls:** These are 20-30 minute sessions with assessors to share context and clarify expectations for findings raised during assessment.
