# Quality Policy

## Policy Statement

Therapeak B.V. is committed to providing safe and effective AI-based therapy software that meets the needs of patients and healthcare professionals, while complying with all applicable regulatory requirements.

We achieve this by:

1. **Safety First** — Ensuring the safety and performance of our medical device software through systematic risk management and rigorous design controls
2. **Regulatory Compliance** — Meeting all requirements of ISO 13485:2016, EU MDR 2017/745, and applicable harmonized standards
3. **Continuous Improvement** — Systematically monitoring, measuring, and improving the effectiveness of our Quality Management System
4. **Customer Focus** — Gathering and acting on feedback from patients, healthcare professionals, and other stakeholders
5. **Competence** — Ensuring all personnel are competent and aware of their contribution to device safety and quality

## Applicability

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and suppliers involved in the design, development, maintenance, and post-market surveillance of our medical devices.

## Review

This policy is reviewed annually during management review ([[SOP-006]]) for continuing suitability.
