## 2. Intended Purpose

Therapeak provides patient-specific supportive conversational guidance intended to help users self-manage mild to moderate mental health symptoms at home.

The device provides:

- AI-powered conversational therapy through text-based sessions with a personalized AI therapist
- Session summaries after each therapy session
- Clinical-style user reports summarizing progress across multiple sessions
- Mood tracking (self-reported and AI-assessed) with visual progress graphs
- Informational coaching content, reflective prompts, and structured coping suggestions

The device output is **informational** in nature. It is not intended to diagnose any condition, establish severity, triage urgency, recommend specific clinical interventions, or determine medication or treatment changes.

## 3. Indications for Use

Therapeak is indicated for use by adults seeking support in self-managing mild to moderate symptoms of:

- Anxiety disorders
- Depression
- Obsessive-compulsive disorders
- Trauma or stress-related disorders
- Disorders related to impulse control

The device may be used standalone or as a supplement to traditional therapy. Reports generated by the device may be shared with a healthcare professional to support consultation preparation, remote monitoring, and follow-up.

## 4. Target User Group

- **Age:** Adults aged 19 and older
- **Clinical profile:** Individuals experiencing mild to moderate mental health symptoms
- **Technical requirements:** Access to a web browser on a computer, tablet, or smartphone with an internet connection
- **Training required:** None — the device is designed for use by lay users without clinical training
- **Setting:** Home use, unsupervised

## 5. Contraindications

**Do NOT use Therapeak if you:**

- Have been diagnosed with, or suspect you may have, complex psychotic or dissociative disorders (e.g., schizophrenia, dissociative identity disorder)
- Are experiencing a mental health emergency or crisis situation
- Have a neurobiological or neurocognitive disorder as the primary condition you wish to address (e.g., dementia, traumatic brain injury) — the device may be less effective for these conditions

If you are unsure whether Therapeak is appropriate for your situation, consult a qualified healthcare professional before use.

## 6. Warnings and Precautions

### 6.1 Important Warnings

- **Therapeak is not a replacement for professional therapy.** It provides AI-generated supportive guidance that may complement, but does not substitute for, care provided by a qualified mental health professional.
- **Therapeak is not for crisis or emergency use.** If you are in a crisis, experiencing thoughts of self-harm, or require immediate help, contact your national crisis helpline, dial emergency services, or visit the nearest emergency room.
- **All content generated by Therapeak is informational, not diagnostic.** The AI does not diagnose conditions, assess severity, or prescribe treatments. Any information provided should be considered as supportive guidance only.
- **You are never obligated to follow AI suggestions.** All guidance provided by the AI therapist is optional. You retain full autonomy over your decisions and actions.
- **All AI therapists are fictional profiles.** The therapist names, biographies, and avatar images are AI-generated. No real people are depicted.

### 6.2 What to Do If You Disagree with AI Guidance

If at any point you disagree with, feel uncomfortable about, or are confused by something the AI therapist says:

1. **Disregard it.** All AI guidance is optional and informational. You are under no obligation to follow any suggestion.
2. **Discuss it with a healthcare professional.** If the AI's output raises questions or concerns, bring them to your doctor, therapist, or other qualified professional.
3. **Switch therapist.** You can switch to a different AI therapist at any time to receive a different therapeutic perspective and style.
4. **Contact support.** If you believe the AI said something harmful, inappropriate, or incorrect, please report it to us at info@therapeak.com so we can investigate and improve the service.

## 7. How to Use Therapeak

### Step 1: Create an Account

1. Visit the Therapeak website.
2. Click "Go To Questionnaire" to begin the onboarding process.
3. Complete the questionnaire (approximately 20 questions covering your background, current concerns, and preferences).
4. Register using your email address and a password, or sign in with a social account (Google, Microsoft, Facebook, or TikTok).
5. Verify your email address using the 4-digit verification code sent to your inbox.

### Step 2: Complete the Questionnaire

The onboarding questionnaire collects information about:

- Your age, gender, and relationship status
- Your previous therapy experience
- Your current mental health concerns
- Symptoms you may be experiencing (using a standardized screening instrument)
- Your preferences for therapist style and focus areas
- A brief description of what brings you to the platform

This information is used to generate a personalized AI therapist matched to your needs and preferences. It is also provided to the AI therapist as context during your sessions.

### Step 3: Start Your First Session

1. After completing the questionnaire, your personalized AI therapist is generated.
2. You receive 10 free trial minutes to experience the service.
3. Begin your session by typing a message in the chat interface.
4. The AI therapist will respond in real time. Sessions are timed and deduct from your minute balance.
5. Sessions have a default allocation of 30 minutes per day (maximum 45 minutes with accumulation).

### Step 4: Review Your Reports and Track Progress

- **Session summaries** are generated automatically after each session, providing a brief overview of what was discussed.
- **User reports** are generated after multiple sessions, providing a structured overview of your presenting concerns, progress, and recommendations.
- **Mood tracking** allows you to record your mood (up to once every 12 hours) and view graphs of your mood over time, including both self-reported and AI-assessed ratings.
- Reports can be exported as PDF and shared with your healthcare professional.

### Step 5: Manage Your Subscription

- Subscribe via the platform to receive a daily minute allocation.
- Additional minutes can be purchased in increments of 15 to 240 minutes.
- You can manage your subscription, payment method, and account settings from your profile.

## 8. Data and Privacy

### 8.1 What Data Is Collected

| Data Category | Examples |
|---|---|
| **Personal data** | Name, email address, date of birth, gender, timezone, locale |
| **Health/wellness data** | Questionnaire responses, full chat transcripts, session summaries, user reports, mood ratings, self-descriptions |
| **Account data** | Social login identifiers, subscription status, payment history |

### 8.2 How Your Data Is Stored

- All user data is stored on servers located in **Nuremberg, Germany** (EU), hosted by Hetzner.
- Data is transmitted securely via SSL/TLS encryption.
- A Data Processing Agreement is in place with the hosting provider.
- Therapy conversation data is transmitted to AI providers (Anthropic Claude via OpenRouter) for processing. Data sharing with third parties for training purposes is disabled.

### 8.3 Your Data Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate personal data
- **Erase** your personal data (right to be forgotten)
- **Restrict** processing of your personal data
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object** to processing of your personal data
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

### 8.4 Account Deletion

- You can delete your account at any time from your profile settings.
- Upon deletion, your account is immediately deactivated (soft delete).
- All associated data is permanently and irreversibly deleted after 180 days.
- If you submit an explicit GDPR erasure request, your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
- To submit an erasure request, contact info@therapeak.com.
