# SOP-001: Document Control Procedure

## 1. Purpose 23

This procedure defines the controls for creating, reviewing, approving, and managing QMS documents.

**ISO 13485 Reference:** Clause 4.2.4

## 2. Scope

Applies to all documents within the Quality Management System.

## 3. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| Author | Draft and revise documents |
| Reviewer | Review for accuracy and completeness |
| Approver | Final approval before release |

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Document Creation

All QMS documents shall follow the standard template and include:
- Document title and ID
- Version number
- Effective date
- Author and approver

### 4.2 Document Review and Approval

Documents shall be reviewed and approved before release. Changes require re-approval.

### 4.3 Document Retention

Documents shall be retained for the lifetime of the medical device, but not less than two years from device release.
