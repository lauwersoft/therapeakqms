# SOP-002: Risk Management Procedure

## 1. Purpose12313213

This procedure defines the risk management process for medical device development in accordance with ISO 14971 and EU MDR Annex I.

**ISO 13485 Reference:** Clause 7.1
**EU MDR Reference:** Annex I, Section 3

## 2. Scope

Applies to all stages of the medical device lifecycle.

## 3. Risk Management Process

### 3.1 Risk Analysis

Identify hazards and estimate associated risks for each device.

### 3.2 Risk Evaluation

Evaluate whether identified risks are acceptable using defined criteria.

### 3.3 Risk Control

Implement risk control measures in order of priority:
1. Inherently safe design
2. Protective measures
3. Information for safety

### 3.4 Overall Residual Risk Evaluation

Evaluate whether the overall residual risk is acceptable.
