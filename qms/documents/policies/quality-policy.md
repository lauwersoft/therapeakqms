# Quality Policy

## Policy Statement

Therapeak B.V. is committed to providing safe and effective AI-based therapy software that supports users in self-managing mild to moderate mental health symptoms. We achieve this through a systematic approach to quality, safety, and continuous improvement.

## Our Commitments

1. **Patient Safety** — We design, develop, and maintain our software with patient safety as the primary consideration. Risks are systematically identified, evaluated, and controlled throughout the product lifecycle.

2. **Regulatory Compliance** — We comply with all applicable requirements of ISO 13485:2016, EU MDR 2017/745, and relevant harmonized standards. We maintain our Quality Management System to support conformity assessment.

3. **Effectiveness** — We monitor the clinical performance of our device through post-market surveillance and act on findings to maintain and improve therapeutic effectiveness.

4. **User Focus** — We listen to user feedback, respond to complaints promptly, and use this information to improve our product and processes.

5. **Continuous Improvement** — We review our QMS regularly, track quality objectives, and take corrective and preventive actions to improve our processes.

## Quality Objectives

| Objective | Measure | Target |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Complaint response | Time to first response | Within 24 hours during normal working hours |
| Serious incidents | Reportable incidents per year | 0 |
| CAPA effectiveness | CAPAs resolved within target timeline | > 80% |
| User satisfaction | Complaint rate vs active users | < 2% |
| Audit readiness | Major nonconformities at audit | 0 |
| Management review | Completed on schedule | Every 6 months |

These objectives are reviewed during management review ([[SOP-006]]) and updated as needed.

## Applicability

This policy applies to all personnel involved in the design, development, maintenance, and post-market surveillance of Therapeak, including the founder, consultants, and any future employees.

## Communication

This policy is available to all relevant personnel through the QMS platform and is reviewed annually during management review.
