# Quality Manual

## 1. Purpose

This Quality Manual describes the Quality Management System (QMS) of [Company Name] in accordance with ISO 13485:2016 and EU MDR 2017/745.

## 2. Scope

This QMS applies to the design, development, and maintenance of AI-based therapy software classified as a Class IIa medical device under EU MDR.

## 3. Quality Policy

[Company Name] is committed to:

- Ensuring the safety and performance of our medical device software
- Meeting all applicable regulatory requirements
- Continuously improving the effectiveness of our QMS
- Maintaining compliance with ISO 13485:2016 and EU MDR 2017/745
