## 5. Declaration

Therapeak B.V. hereby declares, under our sole responsibility, that the device identified above:

1. Conforms to the relevant General Safety and Performance Requirements set out in Annex I of Regulation (EU) 2017/745 on medical devices, as demonstrated in the GSPR Checklist ([[CHK-001]])

2. Is in conformity with the provisions of the following applicable standards and guidance documents:

| Standard / Document | Title |
|---|---|
| ISO 13485:2016 | Medical devices — Quality management systems — Requirements for regulatory purposes |
| ISO 14971:2019 | Medical devices — Application of risk management to medical devices |
| IEC 62304:2006+AMD1:2015 | Medical device software — Software life cycle processes |
| IEC 82304-1:2016 | Health software — Part 1: General requirements for product safety |
| IEC 62366-1:2015 | Medical devices — Part 1: Application of usability engineering to medical devices |
| IEC 81001-5-1:2021 | Health software and health IT systems safety, effectiveness and security — Part 5-1: Security |
| ISO/TR 24971:2020 | Medical devices — Guidance on the application of ISO 14971 |
| MDCG 2019-11 | Guidance on qualification and classification of software in Regulation (EU) 2017/745 |
| MDCG 2019-16 rev.1 | Guidance on cybersecurity for medical devices |
| MDCG 2020-1 | Guidance on clinical evaluation (MDR) / Performance evaluation (IVDR) of medical device software |
| MDCG 2020-3 | Guidance on significant changes regarding the transitional provision under Article 120 of MDR |
| MDCG 2020-5 | Guidance on clinical evaluation — Equivalence |
